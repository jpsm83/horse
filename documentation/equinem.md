## Activity Planning

**Plan with ease anytime from anywhere**

- real-time status of progress and potential delays
- clear communication with staff and riders
- activity reports and insight into horse development over time
- automatic reminders to never forget a vaccination, worm cure or farrier treatment

With a simple and intuitive drag-and-drop interface you can easily plan the desired activities for all your horses and staff members. Track your progress during the day by checking off activities once these are performed. Using EquineM you immediately have an overview of all weekly activities in your stable at a glance, conveniently sortable and filterable. In addition, EquineM also offers monthly overviews per horse, in which all activities and treatments of the relevant horse are visible. This is for example very helpful when creating invoices.Are you a veterinarian with standardized treatment plans for specific injuries? Or are you a rider with default training schedules to get your horse prepared for a competition? Use EquineM to define standard training schedules or treatment plans. These can be re-used and added to the activity planning of a specific horse with a few simple clicks.Automatic reminders ensure that you or your staff never forget a vaccination, worm cure or farrier treatment.

## Team Management

**Clear schedules for everyone on the team**

- availability planning for staff
- dedicated log-in account for each team member
- each individual only sees the information relevant for them in the weekly and daily overview of their account.
- role based permissions management for each account

With EquineM you keep track of your personnel planning in a handy overview that clearly shows who is present and when. Via the reporting module one can always retrieve the historic availability and activity of the staff.

Create or link as many persons to your organization by creating extra accounts. For example you might want to add grooms, riders, vets, farriers, customers, dentists, trainers or even horse owners to your organization for easy scheduling or communication purposes.

For big stables it is easy to lose the overview. EquineM has got you covered with horse groups. It is, for example, possible to link a group of horses to a rider, so that this rider only sees these horses on the weekly and daily overviews.

Also, each account can be assigned to teams and roles, for which granular permission levels can be defined. This way you make sure everyone has access to the relevant information for their role and can make all the required changes.

## Facility Planning

**Online access to occupancy and ability to make reservations directly**

- real-time insight into the occupancy of all facilities
- reserve the paddock, horse walker, riding arena, or other services from home
- set automatic restrictions for reservations for each facility, such as opening and closing hours and maximum number of horses

Facility planning provides a reservation system for all your facilities. It allows your customers to schedule their horses online for the services your stable offers.

Convenient, simple and practical. Insightful and clear. Your customers can use their computer or cell phone at home to view occupancy and schedule reservations for such things as lessons, paddocks, riding boxes, lunging rings, horse walker and any other services.

For each facility, you can also define reservation/scheduling restrictions, such as opening and closing hours. Other possible restrictions include the maximum number of horses that can use the service/facility at the same time, the minimum/maximum reservation time, or the maximum number of days people can plan ahead.

In this way, facility occupancy is immediately visible to everyone and we make reservations simple, fair and user-friendly.
